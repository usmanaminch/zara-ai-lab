About Me

I'm Zara. I build AI tools, write research papers, and explore what happens when technology meets medicine.

This site is my living portfolio — everything here is something I actually made, researched, or shipped. I started this AI lab because I learn best by building real things. That's still true.

I'm interested in how AI can make healthcare more accessible and more equitable — for patients who look different, live in different places, and have different levels of access to care. My projects and writing keep coming back to that question.
